About Coach

Legendary electronic warfare on the RC-135—now sharing the world below

“Coach” earned his name as a legendary electronic warfare officer on the RC-135, where he spent years in the mission that defined a generation of airborne reconnaissance. That same eye for detail and love of the mission now drives something different: bringing people into the hidden world of old military infrastructure.

He gives tours of historic military buildings underground—spaces most people never see. You get the stories, the context, and a guide who’s lived the culture and knows what these places meant when they were in use.
